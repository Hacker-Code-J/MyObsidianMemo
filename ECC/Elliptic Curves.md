What is the number of balls that may be piled as a square pyramid and also re-arranged into a square array?

**Sol:** Let $x$ be the height of the pyramind. Thus,

$$
1^2+2^2+\cdots+x^2=\frac{x(x+1)(2x+1)}{6}.
$$
